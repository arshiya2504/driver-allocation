05 — Key Insights

Analytical Findings — Simulation & Optimisation

  • ↓
    Optimized allocation reduces average cost by approximately 80%, from 673.95 to 132.92 per run, generating average savings of 541 units per scenario.
  • ↑
    Average service level improves by 19.4 percentage points, increasing from 53.6% to 73.0% under the optimised policy.
  • ✓
    The optimised policy performs better in 98.9% of Monte Carlo scenarios, indicating that the result is robust rather than incidental.
  • ~
    Cost variability is dramatically reduced under optimisation, with standard deviation falling from 165.54 to 0.057 and coefficient of variation dropping from 0.246 to 0.000427.
  • ◆
    The optimised policy remains robust under demand uncertainty, with substantially lower tail risk in the worst 10% of scenarios — average cost of 273.25 compared to 950.52 under equal allocation.
